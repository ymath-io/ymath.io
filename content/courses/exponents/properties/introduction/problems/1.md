@statement
Evaluate $2^4$.

@hint
Remember that our ultimate goal is to substitute $x$ for $0$ when we can. Is there anything preventing us from doing so?

@solution
We can plug in $x=0$ to the function, and see that
$$\lim_{x\to 0} x+1=0+1=1.$$
