@statement
Expand and simplify $a(bcd)^e$.

@hint
Group variables and apply the Power of a Product Rule twice.

@solution
We first apply the Power of a Product Rule on $b$ and $cd$, then on 
$$a(bcd)^e
= a(b(cd))^3
= a(b^3(cd)^3)
=a()$$
