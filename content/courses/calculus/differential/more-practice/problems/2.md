$$e^x \rightarrow g(x)$$

$$f(x)\rightarrow f(x)$$

$$f\left(x\right)e^x+f'\left(x\right)e^x$$

$$=\boxed{e^x\left[f\left(x\right)+f'\left(x\right)\right]}$$
