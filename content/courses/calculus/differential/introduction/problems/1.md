@statement
What is the general formula for slope?

@solution
To find the slope, we take rise over run, which is
$$\frac{\text{Change in } y}{\text{Change in } x}.$$

In math, "change in" is denoted as $\Delta$. So, we write
$$\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}.$$
